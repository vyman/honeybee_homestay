About Us

Nestled in the heart of Chikmagaluru, Karnataka's coffee capital, Honeybee Homestay offers a perfect blend of comfort, nature, and authentic local hospitality.

Surrounded by lush coffee plantations and misty mountains, our homestay provides a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Wake up to the aroma of fresh coffee, enjoy breathtaking views, and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the Western Ghats.

Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or a cultural experience, Honeybee Homestay is your home away from home in Chikmagaluru.
